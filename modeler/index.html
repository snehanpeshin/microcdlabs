Before launching

This alpha modeler is for research, concept design, education, and early feasibility work. Designs are not validated for clinical use, manufacturing release, regulatory submission, or patient-facing decisions. Review all geometry, materials, tolerances, and assay requirements with qualified engineers before fabrication or use.
