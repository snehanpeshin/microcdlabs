Privacy Policy

MicroCD Labs Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

MicroCD Labs, a division of Karigari Home LLC, respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use this website, request a quote, or contact us about microfluidics parts, equipment, and related services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the information you choose to provide, including your name, email address, phone number, organization, quote request details, selected catalog items, shipping or billing details, and any technical notes you include in a message.

The current website uses email links for quote and contact requests. When you click those links, your email application may prepare a message using the details you entered on the page.

How We Use Information

We use information to respond to inquiries, prepare quotes, discuss product availability, support customer orders, keep business records, improve our catalog, and comply with legal, tax, export, or safety obligations.

How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers, suppliers, shipping carriers, payment or accounting providers, professional advisors, or government authorities when needed to operate the business, fulfill a request, or comply with applicable law.

Payments Through Stripe

If you pay through a Stripe-hosted invoice, payment link, or checkout page, Stripe processes payment details, billing information, fraud-prevention signals, tax information, and transaction records. MicroCD Labs does not collect or store full card numbers on this website. Stripe's own terms and privacy practices apply to information processed by Stripe.

Cookies and Analytics

This website uses Google tags with default consent settings and may use basic site functionality such as a temporary coupon dialog preference. If additional analytics, advertising pixels, or other tracking tools are added later, this policy should be updated to describe those tools and any choices available to visitors.

Data Retention

We keep business contact, quote, and order information for as long as needed for customer support, business operations, recordkeeping, compliance, and dispute resolution. We try to keep only the information reasonably needed for those purposes.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards appropriate for a small supplier business. No website, email, or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Choices

You can ask us to update, correct, or delete your contact information, subject to business recordkeeping and legal obligations. You can also ask us not to use your email for non-essential follow-up messages.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for business, research, and professional users. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Visitors

If you contact MicroCD Labs from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States. International orders may require additional information for customs, export, sanctions, or end-use review.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our website, catalog, ordering process, or legal obligations change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new last-updated date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact MicroCD Labs at info@microcdlabs.com.
